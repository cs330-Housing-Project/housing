About Us

We are a team of Northwestern computer science students with a simple mission: to help other students find housing solutions with ease.

Housing at Northwestern is a challenge for many. The university provided housing is not only quite expensive, but does not cater to everyones needs. As a result, most students attempt to find off-campus housing for their Junior and Senior years. Unfortunately finding quality housing off campus that meets ones criteria is not always an easy feat. It can be an exhausting process of many google searches, phone calls, disorganized group discussions, house tours, and much more.

With our site, this process becomes a matter of a few clicks.
